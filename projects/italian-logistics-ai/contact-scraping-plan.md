# Zero-Cost MVP Contact Scraping Plan

**Goal:** Build an initial list of 50-100 decision-makers in the Italian regional transport sector (Lombardy & Veneto) to validate the "Logistics AI Dispatcher" offer without upfront software costs.

## Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) for MVP
- **Locations:** Italy - Lombardy (Milano, Brescia, Bergamo) & Veneto (Verona, Vicenza).
- **Keywords (LinkedIn/Google):** "Autotrasporti", "Spedizioni", "Trasporti su strada", "Logistica".
- **Target Titles:** Titolare, CEO, Responsabile Logistica, Fleet Manager.

## Free / Zero-Cost Tool Stack
1.  **Google & LinkedIn Free Search (Prospecting):** Advanced Boolean queries to find local companies.
2.  **Hunter.io / Apollo.io (Free Tiers):** For finding the email format of a company (e.g., `nome.cognome@azienda.it`) - both offer free monthly credits (25-50/mo).
3.  **Google Sheets (Data Entry):** Manual entry of names, companies, and generated emails.
4.  **NeverBounce / Bouncer (Free Credits):** Sign up for free trials to verify the small list of constructed emails (usually ~100 free credits upon signup).
5.  **Gmail / Outlook (Sending):** Sending the emails manually or using a free mail merge add-on like *Yet Another Mail Merge (YAMM)* free tier (50 emails/day).

## Execution Steps (The "Scraping Process")

### 1. Manual Lead Discovery
Use Google Maps and search queries like:
`"autotrasporti" OR "logistica" AND "Brescia" OR "Verona"`
Visit the company websites to see if they fit the profile (fleet size ~10-50 trucks).

### 2. Identify Decision Makers
For selected companies, go to LinkedIn. Search the company name and look at "People". Find the "Titolare" or "Responsabile Logistica".
*Alternative:* Look at the "Contatti" or "Chi Siamo" page on their website.

### 3. Construct the Email Address
If you found Mario Rossi at Autotrasporti Rossi Srl (website: *rossi-trasporti.it*), use Hunter.io (free tier) to find the email pattern.
Common Italian SME patterns:
- `info@rossi-trasporti.it` (often goes straight to the owner in small firms)
- `m.rossi@rossi-trasporti.it`
- `mario@rossi-trasporti.it`

### 4. Verification & Sending
*   Run the compiled list of ~50 emails through a free email verifier trial.
*   Once verified, load them into your regular email client (Gmail).
*   Send the sequence drafted in `cold-email-sequence.md`. Send them manually (or via YAMM) in small batches of 10-15 per day to look entirely organic and avoid spam filters.

## Strategy Note on Cost vs. Time
Since we are removing software costs (Apollo/Instantly), we are trading *money for time*. Building a list of 50 solid leads manually might take 2-3 hours, but it will cost $0 and will perfectly validate the offer (if people reply, the pain point is real). If validation succeeds, then we invest in the paid tools to scale to 1,000+ leads.
