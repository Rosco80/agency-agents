# Campaign Strategy: Italian Logistics AI Dispatcher

**Lead Agent:** Growth Hacker
**Objective:** Capture 50+ high-intent leads (Demo Requests) from Italian SME Logistics owners in 14 days.

---

## 1. Target Audience (The "Ideal Customer")
*   **Segment:** Small to Medium Regional Logistics/Trucking companies in North & Central Italy (Lombardy, Veneto, Emilia-Romagna).
*   **Persona:** "The Overwhelmed Owner" (Andrea). Manages dispatch via WhatsApp/Excel, fears rising fuel costs, and is losing sleep over driver retention.
*   **Channels:** LinkedIn (Targeted by Job Title + Industry + Geo), Niche FB Groups (Camionisti Italiani), and Direct Cold Email.

---

## 2. The "Irresistible Offer"
**The Hook:** "Stop guessing your routes. Save 12% on fuel in 30 days or we pay for your next tank."
**Call to Action (CTA):** "Request a 1:1 Profit-Loss Audit & AI Demo."

---

## 3. Landing Page Structure (MVP)
*   **Design System:** Background `#000000` (Pure Black), Accents & Glows `#c4ef17` (Chartreuse).
*   **Language Feature:** Italian/English toggle in the top-right corner.
*   **Hero Section:** High-impact headline targeting fuel/time waste.
*   **The "Bleeding Neck" Section:** 5 icons representing the Phase 0 pain points.
*   **The ROI Calculator:** Interactive slider (Trucks vs. Miles saved).
*   **Social Proof:** "Powered by Autoflow Solutions NEXUS engine."
*   **Lead Form:** Name, Company, Fleet Size, Current Pain Point.

---

## 4. Growth Loops
*   **Referral Bonus:** "Invite another fleet owner for a free 'Security Audit' by our Security Engineer agent."
*   **Retargeting:** Focus on visitors who started the ROI calculator but didn't submit the form.

---

## 5. Success Metrics (KPIs)
*   **Conversion Rate:** > 8% (Visitors to Leads).
*   **CAC Goal:** < €45 per high-intent lead.
*   **Engagement:** > 4% CTR on ad creatives.
