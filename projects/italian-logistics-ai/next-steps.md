Based on the Phase 0 GO decision, you now have two strategic paths to take the Custom AI Dispatcher concept further. You can choose to validate market appetite with a rapid-fire marketing campaign, or dive straight into building a functional MVP to pitch.

*Best if you want to gauge actual demand and collect pre-orders/waitlist signups before writing any code.*
