# System Specification: Italian Logistics AI Marketing Campaign

This specification outlines the requirements for launching the marketing and lead generation campaign for the Italian Logistics Custom AI Dispatcher.

## 1. Primary Objectives
Launch a 14-day digital marketing campaign targeting Regional Logistics/Trucking SMEs in Italy to generate at least 50 Demostration Requests.

## 2. Target Audience
- Italian Logistics Owners / Fleet Managers (10-50+ Trucks)
- Operating regionally (e.g., Lombardy, Veneto)
- Experiencing pain points with manual dispatching (WhatsApp, Excel) and high fuel costs.

## 3. Brand & Language Guidelines
- **Visuals:** Strict adherence to Autoflow Solutions "Dark Mode" aesthetic. Background: `#000000`. Accents and glow effects: `#c4ef17` (Chartreuse).
- **Language:** Dual-track approach. High-level branding (company name, general authority) in English. All sales-driving copy, social posts, and the landing page UI must be toggleable or presented in **Italian** to remove friction for local owners.

## 4. Required Deliverables
1.  **Fully Functional Landing Page:**
    - Must implement the bilingual copy found in `landing-page-copy.md`.
    - Must include a functional language toggle (ITA/ENG).
    - Must match the visual style of the mockup in `assets/mockup_v2.png`.
    - Must include a functional lead capture form.
2.  **Social Media Content Execution:**
    - Execute the 2-week dual-language LinkedIn content calendar described in `marketing-assets.md`.
3.  **Campaign Analytics & Tracking:**
    - Setup of basic tracking for landing page conversions.

## 5. Development Constraints & Standards
1.  **Frontend:** HTML/Tailwind CSS for maximum speed and responsiveness.
2.  **QA Requirements:** All UI elements (especially the language switch and lead form) must be visually verified against the `#c4ef17` brand standard by the Evidence QA agent.
3.  **Deployment:** The landing page must be ready for internet hosting.
