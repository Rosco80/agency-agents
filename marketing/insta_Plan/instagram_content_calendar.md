# Instagram 30-Day Content Mix: Autoflow Solutions

This framework ensures a healthy balance of Trust, Authority, and Community engagement. 

## Content Pillars (The 1/3 Rule)
- **1/3 Authority:** Showcasing our tech, services, and mastery.
- **1/3 Education:** Providing value and "Aha!" moments for SMEs.
- **1/3 Community (Human):** Behind-the-scenes, client stories, and team culture.

## Format Guidelines
- **Reels:** 15-30 seconds, fast cuts, trending (but subtle) audio.
- **Carousels:** 5-7 slides, high-value info on every slide, strong CTA on the last slide.
- **Stories:** Authenticity over polish. Use captions, interactive stickers, and links to the website.
