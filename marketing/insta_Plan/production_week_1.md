## Day 1: The "SME Revenue Leak" (Reel)
**Goal:** Establish authority and identify a common pain point.

### Video Concept
A high-kinetic video showing a modern office or restaurant where "digital coins" are literally leaking out of outdated systems (spreadsheets, paper, phone lines), followed by a clean, automated "AutoFlow" system stopping the leak.

### High-End AI Video Prompts

#### Option 1: Sora 2 (Cinematic & Professional)
> **Prompt:** A high-end cinematic 4k video of a modern, slightly cluttered restaurant office. On a sleek wooden desk, an old tablet displays a complex Excel spreadsheet. Suddenly, glowing neon lime green coins (#D4FF00) begin to pour out of the screen onto the desk and floor like a digital waterfall. The camera pans out to show a person looking stressed. Then, a translucent "AutoFlow" neon green shield pulses and instantly absorbs the coins, turning the room into a calm, perfectly organized space with floating glassmorphism UI elements showing "Efficiency: 98%". Lighting is dramatic, depth of field is shallow, tech-future aesthetic.

#### Option 2: Nano Banana (Whimsical & Viral)
> **Whimsy Injector Prompt:** A playful, high-energy video in the style of a viral TikTok trend. A businessman is walking down a hallway carrying a stack of overflowing "Spreadsheet" papers. He suddenly slips on a glowing, neon lime green banana peel labeled "Manual Admin." As he falls in slow motion, the papers turn into a swarm of digital yellow bananas that start flying away (labeled "Revenue"). Just before he hits the ground, a robotic AutoFlow hand catches him, stabilizes him, and converts all the flying bananas into a neat pile of gold bars in his briefcase. The background turns from a boring grey office into a vibrant, neon lime tech paradise. 8k, hyper-realistic but stylistically playful, vibrant colors.

### Reel Hook (On-Screen Text)
"Is your SME leaking revenue without you knowing? 🛑"
