# Instagram Engagement & Growth Tactics: Autoflow Solutions

With 15 followers and 70 posts, the priority is **Outbound Engagement (Discovery)** and **Humanizing the Brand (Trust)**. Posting high-quality content is only 50% of the battle; the rest is interaction.

## 1. The "Dollar-Eighty" Strategy (Outbound)
Every day, find the top 10 hashtags relevant to F&B and Real Estate (e.g., `#PropTech`, `#RestaurantMarketing`). 
- On the top 9 posts for each hashtag, leave a **genuine, 3-sentence comment.**
- Avoid "Nice post!" or "🔥🔥🔥." Instead, add value: *"Love how you're highlighting property features here. Have you tried [Specific Automation Tip] to streamline this?"*
- **Target:** 90 comments per day. This drives traffic back to your profile.

## 2. Industry "Sniper" Engagement
Identify key players in your target niches:
- **F&B:** Local restaurant group owners, hospitality consultants, famous chefs.
- **Real Estate:** Top-performing brokers in your target region, property management software companies.
- **Action:** Turn on "Post Notifications" for 10 "Sniper" accounts. Comment within the first 10 minutes of their posts. These comments are highly visible to their (your) target audience.

## 3. The "Human-in-the-Loop" Reels
Instagram rewards "original" video content over stock/AI video.
- **Talking Head:** 15-second clips of you (or a team member) explaining one specific solution.
- **Screen Recording:** Show the "behind the scenes" of a logic flow or a chatbot conversation. Use voiceover to explain the benefits.
- **Office Pulse:** Quick clips of the team working or a celebration of a client win. People buy from people.

## 4. Collaborative Posts (Growth Hack)
Whenever you feature a client or a partner tool, use the **Instagram "Collaborator" feature.**
- This puts the post on **both** feeds, doubling your reach instantly.
- Ask your clients to be collaborators on their "Before/After" transformation posts.

## 5. DM Direct Outreach (Warm Leads)
When an SME owner likes your post or follows you:
- **Don't sell immediately.**
- **Do send a voice note or video DM:** *"Hey [Name], saw you followed us! I love what you're doing with [Their Business]. If you ever want to chat about saving some time on [Specific Painted Point], I'm here. Have a great day!"*
- This personal touch builds massive trust compared to a bot.

## 6. Community Rituals
- **"Automated Fridays":** A recurring Story series where you share one "quick win" automation tip.
- **"SME Spotlight":** Highlighting a business that is working smart (not just hard).
