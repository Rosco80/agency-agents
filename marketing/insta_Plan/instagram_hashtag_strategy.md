# Instagram Hashtag Strategy: Autoflow Solutions

A strategic mix of hashtags to maximize reach across AI technology, SME business, and niche industries (F&B and Real Estate).

## 1. The Strategy: 3-Tier Mix
We use a 30-hashtag maximum (though 15-20 is often optimal for clarity). Every post should use this ratio:

- **Tier 1: Industry-Wide (5-10 hashtags):** High volume (100k+ posts) for broad reach.
- **Tier 2: Niche-Specific (5-10 hashtags):** Medium volume (10k-100k posts) for targeted reach.
- **Tier 3: Branded/Unique (2-3 hashtags):** Low volume for community building.

## 3. Best Practices
- **Mix it up:** Don't use the exact same block of hashtags for every post; Instagram's algorithm can flag it as spam.
- **Hide the block:** Put hashtags in the first comment or use 5 dots/newlines to separate them from the caption.
- **Location Tags:** Always tag your city/region to attract local SMEs.
- **Collaborative Tags:** Tag partners or featured clients in the hashtags (e.g., `#PastryShopTech`).

## 4. Performance Tracking
- **Check Insights:** Every 30 days, identify which hashtags are driving "Reach from Hashtags" and swap out those that aren't performing.
- **Follow Branded Hashtags:** Follow `#AutoflowSolutions` and `#SimplifyAutomateThrive` to interact with users using them.
