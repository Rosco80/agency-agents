# Instagram Brand Aesthetic Guide: Autoflow Solutions

This guide defines the visual identity for the Autoflow Solutions Instagram account to ensure a premium, cohesive, and trust-building presence.

## 1. Color Palette

The secondary "Neon Lime" is our signature energy. It should be used for emphasis and high-tech accents, while the dark palette maintains professionalism.

- **Neon Lime:** `#C4EF17` (Used for CTAs, highlights, and icons)
- **Deep Slate:** `#0F172A` (Primary background for text-heavy posts)
- **Neural White:** `#F8FAFC` (Used for clean typography and high contrast)
- **Cyber Grey:** `#64748B` (Used for secondary info and UI elements)

## 2. Typography

- **Headlines:** Clean, bold Sans-Serif (e.g., Montserrat or Inter Bold).
- **Body Text:** Modern, readable Sans-Serif (e.g., Inter Regular).
- **Micro-Copy:** Monospaced font for a "code-like" feel (e.g., JetBrains Mono) used sparingly for tech details.

## 3. Visual Identity & Imagery Style

We are moving away from purely AI-generated "robot" art and toward a "Human-in-the-Loop" aesthetic.

- **Primary Style:** Clean, high-resolution lifestyle photography of professional workspaces, featuring modern tech (MacBooks, sleek offices).
- **Overlays:** Use Neon Lime line art, glassmorphism containers, and subtle tech-inspired gradients.
- **Human Touch (Priority):** Authentic photos/videos of the team or clients. Avoid generic stock photos of people "shaking hands."
- **Niche Focus:**
  - **F&B:** High-quality food/restaurant shots with automation overlays (e.g., a tablet showing AI reservations).
  - **Real Estate:** Modern property visuals with "Smart Tech" callouts.

## 4. Feed Grid Strategy (9-Post Preview)

Maintain a balanced 1:1:1 ratio across the grid to keep it visually engaging:

1. **The Visionary Reel:** A talking head or client transformation (Movement).
2. **The "Stat" Post:** A bold Neon Lime graphic with a high-impact number (Authority).
3. **The Micro-Case Study:** A carousel showing a "Before/After" of a client's workflow (Social Proof).
4. **The "Workflow" Tip:** Clean UI layout showing an automation logic (Educational).
5. **The Service Highlight:** High-res photo of the tech in action (Product).
6. **The "Reality Check":** A text-based quote or controversial industry opinion (Community).
7. **The "Behind the Scenes":** Office vibe or meeting (Humanize).
8. **The "Quick Win":** A Reel of a specific AI feature in 15 seconds (Value).
9. **The Collaborative Post:** Industry news or partnership feature (Growth).

## 5. Story Aesthetic

- **Highlights Icons:** Simple Neon Lime icons on Deep Slate backgrounds (e.g., 🌮 for F&B, 🏠 for Real Estate, 🤖 for Services).
- **Stickers/Interactive:** Use the native Instagram "Poll," "Question," and "Slider" stickers but within Neon Lime branded boundaries.
